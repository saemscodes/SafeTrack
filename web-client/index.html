Frequently Asked Questions about Calendar

Q: Does Calendar work offline?
A: Yes. Calendar saves your events locally and syncs when connectivity returns.
Q: Do I need an account to use Calendar?
A: No account is required to view and use the calendar. Optional features are available with an account.
Q: Can I share my schedule with contacts?
A: Yes. Calendar supports sharing your availability and coordinating plans with trusted contacts.
Q: Does Calendar work in low-bandwidth areas?
A: Yes. Calendar is designed to work on slow connections and in areas with intermittent internet access.
Q: Is Calendar available as a mobile app?
A: Calendar works as a Progressive Web App (PWA) and can be installed directly from your browser on iOS and Android.
